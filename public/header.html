About Us

Our Mission

At Grace Community Church, our mission is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, and loving service. We are a welcoming, multi-generational church family committed to spiritual growth and community outreach.

  • Christ-centered worship and teaching
  • Vibrant children and youth ministries
  • Community outreach and missions
  • Supportive small groups and Bible studies

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 10:00 AM for uplifting worship, inspiring messages, and fellowship.

Bible Study

Weekly Bible studies for all ages to deepen your understanding of God's Word and grow in faith.

Small Groups

Connect with others in small groups for prayer, support, and spiritual growth throughout the week.

Children's Ministry

Fun, safe, and engaging programs for children to learn about Jesus and build lasting friendships.

Community Outreach

Serving our neighbors through food drives, volunteering, and local missions projects.

Worship Team

Join our worship team and use your musical gifts to glorify God and lead others in praise.
